## Hello folks, this is Vipul  <img src="https://docs.mixster.dev/img/hatching.gif" height=25 width=25 />

I am a software engineer building hard-tech products in the open, scalable systems in resource-constrained environments, and sustaining open-source communities for a decade. I was recognized as a [GitHub Star](https://stars.github.com/profiles/vipulgupta2048/) in 2025.

At [balena](https://balena.io), we built Leviathan, an open-source e2e hardware automation jig to test embedded operating systems directly on edge devices, drastically improving reliability and fast-tracking OS release cycles from 6 weeks to 3 hours, saving tens of thousands of dollars.

Learning as I go, I have worn multiple hats at work and excel at working in uncertain, challenging scenarios. 

Apart from my full-time commitment, I run [Mixster](https://bit.ly/whatmixster), an initiative to enable startups to "right" their docs, document their engineering culture, and create scalable content pipelines from scratch. Mixster is made up of `engineers who love writing docs`.

Offline, I volunteer to put together PyCon India as Content Lead, [OpenHiL](https://github.com/openhil/openhil.github.io) for hardware in the loop developers, run a 3000+ members student community [ALiAS](https://asetalias.in) to mentor early stage developers, and organize GitHub's [GitTogether India community](https://mixster.dev/2025/07/11/github-gittogether-delhi-meetup/). Read more about [my communities](https://docs.mixster.dev/Communities).

I am a documentarian in life too and have been [documenting my life](http://mixster.dev/), [work](https://docs.mixster.dev/), and [travel](https://mixster.dev/lets-goto/) extensively.
